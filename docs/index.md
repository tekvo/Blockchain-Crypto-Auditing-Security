1. **Types of blockchain attacks** — Threat landscape at the chain, wallet, and contract layers.
2. **Smart contract attacks** and **Blockchain best practices** — Contract bugs paired with defensive patterns.
3. **Web3 security tools** — Tooling and formal methods pointers.
4. **EVM low-level coding** — EVM perspective when reviewing bytecode or gas behavior.
5. **Audit roadmap** — Practical internal audit checklist before external review.
6. **Token due diligence** and **Audit reports** — Token screening and how professional reports are structured.
7. **Secureum** — Broader Web3 and Eth2 security reading.
