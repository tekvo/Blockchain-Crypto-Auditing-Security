## Industry overview and key challenges

| Challenge | Business impact | How Tekvo fits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Economy exploits** | Duplication bugs destroy trust overnight. | **Invariant testing** for item and currency flows. |
| **Botting and RMT** | On-chain markets attract automated abuse. | **Rate limits**, **signatures**, and **game server** trust boundaries. |
| **Platform fees and royalties** | Players perceive hidden drains as fraud. | **Transparent fee** and **admin** review. |
| **Cross-game interoperability** | Composable items increase attack surface. | **Bridge and metadata** review. |

## AI and technology adoption in the industry

Teams are adopting **AI for documentation, code assist, and operations**—which accelerates delivery but can skip explicit invariants and cross-party trust analysis. AI assists **NPC behavior and moderation**; tying rewards to chain txs needs **anti-sybil** thinking when bots can farm.

**Tekvo’s stance:** pair AI acceleration with **written assumptions, tests, and human review** at integration boundaries—especially where blockchain touches custody, identity, or settlement.
