## AI and technology adoption in the industry

Teams are adopting **AI for documentation, code assist, and operations**—which accelerates delivery but can skip explicit invariants and cross-party trust analysis. Generative tools speed **creative and contract** iteration; without checklist discipline, **misconfigured royalties** and **metadata** bugs ship fast.

**Tekvo’s stance:** pair AI acceleration with **written assumptions, tests, and human review** at integration boundaries—especially where blockchain touches custody, identity, or settlement.
