## AI and technology adoption in the industry

Teams are adopting **AI for documentation, code assist, and operations**—which accelerates delivery but can skip explicit invariants and cross-party trust analysis. Route and demand forecasting AI influences **pricing oracles**; guard against **feedback loops** that hurt fairness or safety margins.

**Tekvo’s stance:** pair AI acceleration with **written assumptions, tests, and human review** at integration boundaries—especially where blockchain touches custody, identity, or settlement.
